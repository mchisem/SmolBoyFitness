Conditioning for Strength

Making progress with your training takes a lot of time. You have to lay out a plan that works for you and adapts to your life, regularly check in on what the data from each week shows you to make adjustments, and have backup plans for when life throws a wrench at your training. If you value your training and making progress on your goals but don’t have the time to develop and implement your own plan, this coaching option is perfect for you.


SBF General Coaching is coaching and programming for anyone who wants to learn the basics of lifting, or who already understands the basics of strength training and wants to make progress on their strength/lifting goals, but without worrying about the intricacies of nutrition, weight change, etc. Programming is designed using the latest research in exercise science and experiential data. Together we will build you a lifting program that is optimal within the context of your life, progresses at your own pace, and adapts to your needs and recovery. We’ll also have regular check-ins to see how everything is going and make changes as necessary.


Coaching is about much more than just changing spreadsheets every couple of weeks. I'm here to build a genuine relationship with you and provide you with support, guidance, mentoring, and education. I truly believe that we can both learn so much from one another, and in doing so, we'll work together to set reasonable goals and expectations, and work toward them in a healthy, safe, and sustainable way.

Who is it for?

  • Beginner and advanced lifters alike who want to learn how to improve their skills in the gym and take their training to the next level
  • Lifters who want to focus on their goals by honing in on the added facets of nutrition in order to get bigger and/or stronger
  • Lifters who want or need more guidance or support in regards to their lifting program and their plans to meet their goals
  • Lifters who have some training experience but want to learn more about how to build and progress an individualized strength/physique training program, and how to interpret training data and utilize it to make changes
  • Lifters who are interested in potentially competing in a physique show or powerlifting meet
  • Lifters who are interested in focusing on powerlifting at an amateur or recreational level, and build strength in the Big 3 Power Lifts
  • Lifters who are interested in building complete, overall strength in other specific lifts including or outside of just the Big 3 Powerlifts (such as Overhead Press, Rows, Pull-Ups, specific Bench variations, etc.)
  • Anyone at any skill level who wants to build general, full-body, overall strength, but doesn’t know which lifts to start with/focus on
  • Anyone who wants to feel empowered by seeing just how strong your body can truly be and what it’s capable of
  • Anyone interested in pursuing their body’s muscle-building potential, curious about just how far you can push your physique

What do you get?

  • An initial, judgement-free assessment of your current skill and education level when it comes to lifting and strength training (just to see where we’ll be starting from!)
  • A 1-hour video consultation with me to determine your goals, get to know you and your needs, and build your program together
  • A movement screening to see where you’re at with technique, and what changes we need to make to your technique/programming to make your lifting more efficient*
  • An evolving, customized lifting and conditioning program to build strength and/or muscle based on your goals. This will be a spreadsheet-based program and will be tailored to fit your life: your needs, preferences, schedule, access to equipment, ability, skill level, and goals.
  • A complementary nutrition plan and regular assessment of your recovery plan to help support your training in a long-term, sustainable way
  • A folder of resources on how to track your progress and implement the program we build
  • Video or voice message check-ins with me every 2 weeks where you report to me in order to assess progress
  • General coaching does not include individualized nutritional planning, support, advice, or guidance
  • General coaching does not include conditioning guidance or programming to optimize your strength training work capacity and physique goals, including cardio, general daily activity, etc.

*Movement screening is required for all clients regardless of stated skill level in lifting before starting any program. This is essential to ensure your safety before adding on additional load, which could potentially reinforce inefficient or unsafe movement patterns under heavy load. Your program will be designed to address severe technique issues, under light or no load, that might put your safety at risk before loading an exercise with more weight.

**Disclaimer: This is coaching, programming, support, and education for amateur/recreational lifters, or lifters in a specific strength sport (e.g. bodybuilding/physique sports, powerlifting, etc.) If you are looking for strength & conditioning coaching for another sport that involves or is related to resistance training (e.g. American football, wrestling, basketball, etc.), I would strongly recommend hiring a sports-specific coach/trainer, or a certified Strength & Conditioning Coach. If you are looking for a targeted nutrition plan for weight loss/management or for support of non-lifting athletic endeavors, I would strongly recommend hiring a registered dietitian.
